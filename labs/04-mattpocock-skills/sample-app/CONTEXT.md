# decide Context

## Shared Language

- Decision: a small choice the team wants to remember.
- Pending decision: a decision that has been proposed but not accepted.
- Accepted decision: a pending decision that the team has chosen to follow.
- Rationale: the short reason captured with `--why`.

## Current Assumptions

- The decision log is optimized for git review, not for database queries.
- The CLI should keep the markdown readable after every command.
- Numbering in `list` is a view over pending decisions, not a permanent ID.
